Mac'n'Cheese

Thick and saucy Mac'n'Cheese, a simple recipe with a big heart

A family favourite with some variety to add a healthier vibe!

Ingredients

  • 1 x bag of Macaroni Pasta
  • 500g of Streaky Bacon
  • 1 litre of Milk
  • 500g of Cheese
  • 2 x Capsicums
  • 1 x Onion
  • 2 x tablespoons of Flour
  • 200g of Butter

Method

  1. Take a pot and fill with water (enough to cover all of the pasta) and heat on the stove on medium heat till boiling.

  2. While the pot is heating up we will focus on the ingrediants we will fry:

    1. Take the bacon and slice bacon every two finger lengths
    2. Put bacon in pan and fry on high till crispy, stirring every so often
    3. While the bacon gets crispy peel the onion and remove core of the capsicums
    4. Dice the onion and capsicum and add to the bacon in the pan, reduce heat to low
    5. Stir the pan every so often and turn off after the vegetables soften

  3. Add pasta to the pot and set timer for 12 minutes

  4. While the pasta boils, begin to make the cheese sauce in a smaller pot:

    1. Place the butter and heat the pot on low
    2. Add the flour once melted and stir constantly
    3. As it begins to thicken add a small amount of milk
    4. Repeat the previous step multiple untill you get the desired volume of sauce or all the milk is used

      Note: Do not let the sauce get to runny by adding milk to quickly as it will then take a long time to thicken again

    5. Once you have enough volume grate the cheese and add to the sauce, stirring each time
    6. Taste test till desired thickness and cheese flavour

  5. Drain pasta of water and pour into a large bowl, add fried ingredients and then the cheese sauce

  6. Stir together and then serve
