Sausages and Mash

A beautifully easy dinner that will be a favourite of all the kids

Ingredients

  • 6 x sausages
  • 5 x Potatoes
  • 1 x Gravy sachet
  • 2 x Carrot
  • 200g Butter

Method

  1. Take potatoes and cut them into quarters or smaller if you are using large potatoes

  2. While you are here also cut the 2 carrots at 5 millimetre intervals to create small circles

  3. Put the cut potatoes in a pot 3/4 full of water and put them on to boil

  4. While potatos boil, get a frying pan and the sausages out

    1. Add a small amount of oil to the pan and place the sausages in the pan
    2. Turn the heat on to medium and place fork holes in the sausages
    3. Rotate the sausages as needed
    4. Cook till the sausages are a light brown colour

  5. While the sausages cook take out the gravy packet and put the jog on to boil

    1. Put the sachet and boiling water into a measuring jug, following the packets instructions and stir
    2. Once the sausages are cooked put the heat onto low and add the gravy and carrots
    3. Stir the sausages and carrot into the gravy and leave on low to simmer

  6. Check the potatoes and remove from heat once the potatoes are soft

    1. Once soft drain the water from the pot or pour potatoes into a colander
    2. Place 200g of butter into the potatoes and mash the potatoes into the butter
    3. If the mash isnt soft enough for your liking add a small amount of milk and mash again
    4. Finish the mash by using a fork and whipping the mash around to get any left over chunks

  7. After completion serve the mash and sausages together adding extra gravy from the pot to desired locations
